#+title: addressing bluetooth
a unique 48 bit BD_ADDR is assigned to each device
the first 24 bits are the lower address parts
the next 8 bits are the upper address parts
the rest are nonsignificant
this is how addresses work in [[id:4b59427e-21dc-43c7-865a-a1fc6eeb20e8][bluetooth]]
