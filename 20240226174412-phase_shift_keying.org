#+title: phase shift keying
in psk the phase of a constant amplitude carrier is modified to present different symbols
for binary psk two phase values encode the 1 and 0 logic values, typically the difference is 180
this is a way of carrying binary over [[id:c3f36734-ab9f-4660-b15e-a59e3a6ad536][signal transmission]]
