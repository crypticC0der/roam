#+title: attacks
an attack is a finite state machine (normally) that will do a thing every time
that finite state machine is up
there are 4 types of attacks
- ranged
- area
- melee
- close (deprecated)

attacks are also children of [[id:7823d424-596e-4a3d-a32d-cd81a9d35c5c][damagers]]
