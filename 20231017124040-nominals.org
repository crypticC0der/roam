#+title: nominals
Nominal -> Noun | Adj Noun | Num Noun | Nominal Noun ...
Nominal -> Nominal PP
Nominal -> Nominal GerundVP
Nominal -> Nominal RelClause

norminals follow [[id:92985529-268d-45f2-9985-3d4b3829e63a][determiner]]s they contain the head of the [[id:b4b829c4-9d06-4bdd-8504-5e788c8eee68][Noun Phrases]] and then any pre and post modifiers

pre modifers are generally quantifiers cardinals and ordianls, like "big" and "three"

post modifiers are either
- prepositional
  ie "from seattle"

- non finite clauses
  "arriving before noon"

- relative clauses
  "that serve breakfast"
