#+title: ambiguity
you have word level ambiguity and sentence level ambiguity, sentence level is wherin the sentence itself can have multiple interpretations
such as i made her duck,
and word level is where the word has multiple interpretations such as apple

more formally:
- [[id:cc0115ac-4af9-4d67-841c-3951082890c9][lexical ambiguity]] -> a word with multiple aprt of speach tags, ie verb or noun
- lexical semantic mbiguity -> word with different senses
- syntactic ambiguity -> ambiguity from multiple possible word groupings
