#+title: wide personal area network
a wpan is born when a device wants to transmit info and remains alive as long as such request exists
either synchronous connection oriented channel (audio) , or asynchronous connectionless channel (files)
wpan is 2.4ghz (same as wifi), and uses [[id:7ca257a1-118d-4262-82fe-53244dc46a22][frequency hopped spread spectrum]] to minimize interference
